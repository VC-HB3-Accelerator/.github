## Business Pain Points

- Rising costs of SaaS services with paid subscriptions
- Data moved manually between systems
- Sensitive data leaks, theft, breaches
- No IT infrastructure tailored to your business
- No in-house IT staff or budget to hire
- Messy accounting and analytics
- Dependency on service plans and limits
- Meeting regulator requirements for data storage and processing
- Difficulty attracting investment to scale

### Accelerator Program

- Registration in a group by your line of business
- Contractors help configure the platform for your business
- Online sessions, workshops, peer exchange within the group
- Technical support and updates for 5 years

More: [accelerator-program.md](accelerator-program.md)

### Access to Investment

The fund selects top accelerator participants for investment:
- Stages: Pre-seed to ICO
- Check size: 50,000 to 4,000,000 USDT (by stage)
- In exchange for governance tokens of your company
